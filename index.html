Our Profile

Bridge Research and Innovation for Democratic Governance and Enhanced Peace (BRIDGE) is an independent, non-profit think tank based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Registered under the FDRE Civil Society Organization Proclamation No. 1113/2019, we specialize in parliamentary affairs, peace, and development.

Vision

To be a leading research hub and trusted source of analysis on parliamentary affairs, peace, governance, and development in the IGAD region.

Core Values

  • Rigor: High-quality, evidence-driven research.
  • Innovation: Adaptive solutions for governance and peace.
  • Trust: Transparent and reliable analysis.

Key Objectives

We aim to strengthen legislative institutions through:

  • Evidence-based research.
  • Capacity-building for parliamentarians.
  • Monitoring parliamentary accountability.
